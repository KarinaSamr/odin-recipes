Pirozhki s katroshechkoj

Description:

A new modern perspective on classic russian recipe: pirozhki!

This time easy to prepare, delicious and not forgettable! Every babushka would be proud!

Ingredients:

  • Layered, alredy prepared dough
  • Spices of your choice
  • Potatoes
  • Egg
  • Quark
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Cut and boil potatoes until they are ready
  2. Smash potatoes with a fork, the consistency should not be too smooth
  3. Melt butter on the small pan, add the spices
  4. Combine the spices and melt butter together with the smashed potatoes and quark
  5. Cut the dough on similar pieces, aproximately 12x12 cm
  6. Put a couple of spoons of the potatoes inside the dough square and put the ends of the square together
  7. Brush the egg on top of ready pirozhki
  8. Bake with 180Â°C for 40-50 min
