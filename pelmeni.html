Pelmeni

Description:

Delicious pelmeni are especially good when you have no idea what to make and you want something fast, delicious and fulfilling.

Look no further, pelmeni is the way to go. That is if you don't want to make them from scratch.

Ingredients:

  • Store bought pelmeni
  • Butter
  • Spices

Steps:

  • Prepare pelmeni according to the instructions
  • Put on some spices and butter
  • Enjoy!
