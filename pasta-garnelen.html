Pasta with shrimps

Description

Wonderful pasta with delicious cream sauce is easy to make, and very nutricious!

Only a handful of ingridients, some love and just a little bit of your time required to create this masterpiece, that chef Gordon Ramsay would be jealous of!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Cream
  • Zuchini
  • Spinach
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Parmesan
  • Shrimps

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta, according to the instructions
  2. Fry shrimps on the pan
  3. Add garlic and onion, wait until they are ready
  4. Add zuchini, spinach and wait 5 min
  5. Add cream and stir to combine all the ingridients
  6. Add parmesan
  7. Distribute pasta on the plates and put sauce with vegetables and shripms on top
  8. Add parmesan and enjoy!
